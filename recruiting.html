Steps to Get Recruited

  1. Create a strong highlight video
  2. Email college coaches with your information
  3. Attend showcases and camps
  4. Maintain good academic performance

What Coaches Look For

  • Skill level and consistency
  • Work ethic and attitude
  • Baseball IQ
  • Academic eligibility

Emailing Coaches

When reaching out to coaches, include your name, graduation year, position, stats, and a link to your highlight video.

Stay consistent and keep improving every day.
