Welcome to My Website

This website is designed to help baseball players with hopes of playing baseball in college. By assisting in the decision making process as well as helping high school players get their names out there, this website will supply athletes with information about baseball programs. This information will include on campus facilities, academic requirements, baseball program history, coaches name and contact information, as well as player testimonies. By allowing these players to have easy access to information, it can help them navigate the stress inducing process of trying to get recruited and finding the right place for them..
